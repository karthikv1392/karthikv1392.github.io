I am a Ph.D. student in Computer Science at Gran Sasso Science Institute, Italy. I am from Sekharipuram, a village in the city of Palakkad, Kerala, India. Prior to joining GSSI, I was working as a Product Lead / Data Scientist in Knowledge Lens Pvt. Ltd, Bengaluru, India. I was fortunate to get an oppurtunity to receive dual master degrees, An M.Tech in Computer Science specialized in Machine Learning and Data Science from Amrita University, India and MSc in Computer Science specialized in Software Architecture from University of L’Aquila, Italy.

My major research interests include Software Architectures, Model Driven Engineering, Machine Learning and Data Sciecne. I am working under the supervision of Dr. Henry Muccini, Associate Professor, University of L’Aquila, Italy for my Doctoral Thesis.
